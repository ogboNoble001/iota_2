About iota

Who we are and what drives us

Our Mission

iota exists to democratize mobile UI/UX design. We believe that creating beautiful, functional mobile interfaces shouldn't require expensive software or high-end hardware.

We're committed to making design accessible to the next billion creators, especially in regions where traditional design tools are out of reach. Every designer deserves access to professional-grade tools, regardless of their location or economic situation.

By leveraging modern web technologies, we've created a tool that works seamlessly across devices, from smartphones to tablets to desktop computers, ensuring that creativity knows no bounds.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, iota was born from a simple observation: while the world was going mobile-first, design tools were still stuck in the desktop era.

We asked ourselves: what if you could design professional mobile UIs right from your phone? What if it was completely free? That vision became iota.

Our founding team, composed of designers and developers from around the globe, brought together diverse perspectives on what makes design tools truly accessible. We've worked in emerging markets, we've felt the frustration of limited resources, and we've experienced firsthand the transformative power of accessible technology.

Today, iota serves thousands of designers across six continents, proving that great design tools can be both powerful and accessible.

Product Overview

Everything about the iota editor

iota is a comprehensive mobile UI/UX design platform that runs entirely in your browser. Whether you're designing your first app mockup or creating complex interaction flows, iota provides all the tools you need without the complexity of traditional design software.

The Editor

Our powerful browser-based editor delivers desktop-level functionality with a mobile-optimized interface. It features an intuitive canvas that responds beautifully to both touch and mouse input.

The editor supports real-time collaboration, unlimited undo/redo, smart guides for precise alignment, and a clean, distraction-free workspace that lets you focus on what matters: your design.

  • Responsive canvas with zoom controls
  • Layer management and grouping
  • Grid and snap-to-guide systems
  • Keyboard shortcuts for power users

Device Frames

Design with context using our extensive library of device frames. Choose from pre-configured frames for popular smartphones and tablets, or create custom dimensions for any screen size.

Each frame includes accurate screen dimensions, notch positions, and safe areas, ensuring your designs work perfectly on real devices.

  • iPhone 15 Pro, Galaxy S24, Pixel 8
  • iPad Pro, Surface, custom tablets
  • Custom dimensions for any device
  • Automatic safe area indicators

Component Library

Access hundreds of pre-built mobile UI components designed according to iOS and Android design guidelines. Every component is optimized for touch interfaces and can be customized to match your brand.

From basic buttons and inputs to complex navigation patterns and data visualizations, our library accelerates your design workflow without compromising quality.

  • Navigation bars and tab bars
  • Cards, lists, and data displays
  • Forms, inputs, and controls
  • Modals, sheets, and overlays

Design System

Build consistent interfaces with our integrated design system tools. Define your color palette, typography scale, spacing units, and component variants once, then apply them throughout your project.

Changes to your design system automatically propagate across all screens, ensuring consistency and saving hours of manual updates.

Export & Share

Export your designs in multiple formats including PNG, SVG, and PDF. Generate assets at different resolutions for iOS and Android, or create interactive prototypes to share with stakeholders.

Share designs via link, export development-ready specs, or download complete asset packages for handoff to developers.

Offline Support

Thanks to Progressive Web App technology, iota works offline after your first visit. Design on a plane, in a coffee shop without WiFi, or anywhere your creativity strikes.

All your work is saved locally and syncs automatically when you're back online, ensuring you never lose progress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is iota really completely free?

Yes! Iota is 100% free with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or feature limitations. All features are available to all users.

Can I use iota offline?

Absolutely. After your first visit, iota works completely offline thanks to Progressive Web App technology.

Where are my designs stored?

All your designs are stored locally on your device. We don't upload or store your work on our servers.

Can I export my designs?

Yes! Export in PNG, SVG, or PDF formats at any resolution. You can also generate shareable prototype links.

What devices does iota work on?

Iota works on any device with a modern web browser - smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.

Do you offer enterprise features?

We're exploring enterprise options for the future, but the core product will always remain free for everyone.
